What do we do?

Yssen Supply Chain Co. is a China-based international logistics company headquartered in Shanghai. Our comprehensive services in China include sea and air international transportation, break bulk projects, SOC management, customs clearance, warehousing, trucking, feeder delivery, cargo survey, PE, and courier parcels. We collaborate with our esteemed partners and carriers to facilitate cargo transportation to and from Oceania, Europe, America, and Asia.
